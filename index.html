Learning to bridge human-level reasoning with machine intelligence to lead the next wave of adaptive, intelligent systems.

I'm a recent graduate in Data Science with a deep curiosity for how intelligence—both artificial and human—works. My interests lie at the intersection of AI, cognitive psychology, and human-centered design. I'm particularly fascinated by how machines can not only learn, but begin to reason, communicate, and respond in ways that feel meaningfully human. There's something magical about the moment when code transforms into understanding, when algorithms start to mirror the beautiful complexity of how we think and feel. I believe the future of AI isn't just about building smarter systems, but about creating technology that truly understands and enhances the human experience.
